About The Movie

The movie takes place after the events of the first film, with Arthur Fleck, aka Joker, in custody at Arkham State Hospital. Arthur's lawyer plans to argue that he suffers from dissociative identity disorder. At the hospital, Arthur meets Harleen "Lee" Quinzel, a patient who becomes obsessed with him. Lee's obsession with Arthur leads to a deadly romantic relationship between the two. As the story unfolds, Arthur's Joker persona becomes a symbol of chaos and anarchy in Gotham City. The film features a unique blend of music, dance, and drama, with Arthur and Lee breaking into song and dance numbers throughout the movie. The movie's plot thickens as Arthur's trial approaches, with the prosecution seeking to hold him accountable for his crimes. In the end, Arthur's fate is sealed, but not before he inspires a new generation of chaos and anarchy in Gotham City. The movie concludes with a haunting scene, as a young inmate carves a Glasgow smile into his own face, hinting at a new Joker in the making .
